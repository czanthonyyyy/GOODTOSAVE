Data Protection

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Data Protection Notice summarizes our technical and organizational measures ("TOMs") and principles for safeguarding personal data processed by Good to Save.

1. Principles

  • Lawfulness, fairness, transparency
  • Purpose limitation and data minimization
  • Accuracy and storage limitation
  • Integrity, confidentiality, and accountability

2. Technical and Organizational Measures

  • Access control: role-based access, multi-factor authentication for administrative access, least-privilege.
  • Encryption: TLS in transit; encryption at rest where applicable.
  • Logging and monitoring: audit logs for critical events, anomaly detection.
  • Resilience: backups, tested recovery procedures, high availability where appropriate.
  • Secure development: change management, code reviews, dependency management, vulnerability remediation.
  • Vendor due diligence: data processing agreements, SCCs where required.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the specified purposes and legal requirements. We implement scheduled deletion for dormant accounts and expired records where feasible.

4. Incident Response

We maintain procedures to detect, assess, and respond to personal data incidents, including user and authority notifications as required by law.

5. Data Subject Rights

To exercise rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, or objection (as applicable), contact privacy@goodtosave.com.
