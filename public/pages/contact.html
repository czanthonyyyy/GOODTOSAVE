Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs through our contact form by selecting "Report a Bug" as the subject. Please include as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue and screenshots if applicable.

What are your response times?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use our live chat feature or call us directly.

How can I become a partner?

We're always looking for partners who share our vision of reducing food waste. Please contact us with details about your organization and how you'd like to collaborate.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for all our users. You can reach our support team through email, phone, or live chat.
