Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 1, 2025

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We aim to comply with WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and continuously improve the user experience for everyone.

Measures to Support Accessibility

  • Semantic HTML and ARIA where appropriate
  • Keyboard navigability and focus management
  • Contrast, color, and text scaling considerations

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at accessibility@goodtosave.com.
