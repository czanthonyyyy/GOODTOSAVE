Leftover night

Turn “bits and bobs” into a show‑stopping bowl

How to build the perfect bowl

  • Base: rice, grains, noodles or greens.
  • Color: roast any veggie mix 12–15 minutes.
  • Protein: eggs, beans, tofu, leftover chicken or fish.
  • Sauce: yogurt + lemon, tahini, or quick vinaigrette.
  • Crunch: toasted nuts, seeds, or croutons (Day‑2 bread!).
