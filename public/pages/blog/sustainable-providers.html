Sustainable Providers: Our Selection Criteria

Categories: Sustainability, Community

Published on March 2, 2025

We partner with providers who commit to food safety, quality standards, and environmentally responsible practices. Here is how we evaluate and onboard new partners to the Good to Save marketplace.

What we look for

  • Compliance with food safety regulations.
  • Responsible surplus management and transparency.
  • Commitment to community impact and sustainability goals.
