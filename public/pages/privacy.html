Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Good to Save ("we", "us", "our") collects and processes personal data when you use our websites, apps, products, and services (collectively, the "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information in a lawful, fair, and transparent manner.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Good to Save. You can contact us at privacy@goodtosave.com for privacy-related inquiries.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to personal data we process about consumers, visitors, and account holders located in the EU/EEA and other jurisdictions where we operate. Additional rights may apply depending on your location.

3. Categories of Personal Data

  • Identification and contact data: name, email address, phone (optional), authentication identifiers.
  • Account and profile data: preferences, saved items, communication settings.
  • Transaction data: orders, purchase history, payment method tokens (processed by payment providers), amounts, timestamps.
  • Usage and device data: IP address, device/browser info, pages viewed, referral, session and interaction events, cookies and similar technologies.
  • Support communications: messages and attachments you send to our support team.

4. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Provide the Services (create/manage your account, process orders, enable pickup/QR codes) — Art. 6(1)(b) performance of a contract.
  • Payments and fraud prevention — Art. 6(1)(b) contract and Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interests.
  • Service improvement and analytics (e.g., usage metrics) — Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interests. Where required, we obtain consent for non-essential cookies — Art. 6(1)(a).
  • Customer support and communications (service notices, transactional emails) — Art. 6(1)(b) contract.
  • Marketing communications (newsletters, offers) — Art. 6(1)(a) consent. You can opt out at any time.
  • Legal compliance (tax, accounting, law enforcement requests) — Art. 6(1)(c) legal obligation.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, enhance performance, and perform analytics. Where required by law, we request your consent. You can manage preferences via your browser and device settings.

6. Data Sharing and Recipients

We share personal data with:

  • Payment processors to process transactions securely.
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, communications) who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Business partners/providers to fulfill your orders and enable pickup/collection.
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights, users, and the public.

7. International Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as adequacy decisions, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), and supplementary measures as needed.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Criteria include account status, legal obligations, and dispute resolution needs.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (e.g., GDPR), you have the right to request: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and to object to certain processing. Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing. To exercise rights, contact privacy@goodtosave.com. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., the Spanish Data Protection Agency, AEPD).

10. Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child provided us personal data, contact us to delete it.

11. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data (encryption in transit, access controls, monitoring). No method is 100% secure; we work to continually improve our safeguards.

12. Automated Decision-Making

We do not engage in automated decision-making producing legal or similarly significant effects without human involvement.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated version with a revised "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you by email or in-product message.

14. Contact

Questions or requests about this Policy: privacy@goodtosave.com.
