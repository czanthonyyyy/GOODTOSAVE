Our Mission

At FoodSaver, we believe every bite counts. Our mission is to create a world where no food goes to waste, connecting conscious consumers with providers committed to sustainability.

The Food Waste Problem

Every year, more than 1.3 billion tons of food are wasted worldwide. This not only represents an economic loss of 1 trillion dollars, but also contributes significantly to climate change, generating 8% of global CO2 emissions.

Our Solution

We have created a platform that directly connects consumers with providers who have surplus high-quality food. This allows food to reach family tables instead of ending up in landfills.
